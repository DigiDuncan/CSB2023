"Don't underestimate me."

A defector from a violent cult who grew disgusted with their clan's practices. While Tate is physically weak, they do possess some magical abilities. After being ostracized since childhood for protesting every conquest undertaken by the cult and for being unable to ever win in a "real" fight, out of fear for their life, Tate fled.

Tate now strives to lead a peaceful existence while posing as a human. Although they are a pacifist at heart, sometimes the echoes of the past become too much to bear...
