He's a pirate by blood and a lover of trains by heart. Mean has the ability to swap between this and his sea urchin form at will. Now that he's got a new job that he can be proud of, he wants to present as human a little more often.

Because Mean completed all of his job training as a sea urchin, however, it has been taking some time for his coworkers to still be able to recognize him. He hopes that wearing the one-of-a-kind hat that Tate gave him will help ease this transition.
